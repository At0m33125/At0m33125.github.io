Reading Comprehension Study Across Age Groups

Participant Instructions

Thank you for participating in this science fair project on reading comprehension. Your contribution will help us understand various aspects of reading across different age groups.

What You'll Be Doing:

  1. Pre-Test Questionnaire: A brief survey about your reading habits and background.
  2. Reading Tasks: You'll read several short passages on a computer screen.
  3. Comprehension Quizzes: After each passage, you'll answer a few multiple-choice questions.
  4. Post-Test Survey: A short questionnaire about your experience with the reading tasks.

Important Instructions:

  • Read each passage at your natural pace - this isn't a speed reading contest.
  • Try to understand the content as you would normally when reading.
  • Answer the comprehension questions to the best of your ability.
  • If you wear glasses or contact lenses for reading, please wear them during the test.
  • Feel free to take short breaks between passages if needed.
  • There are no "right" or "wrong" ways to read - we're interested in your natural reading behavior.

Safety Considerations:

  • You'll be reading from a computer screen. To minimize eye strain:
    • The screen will be positioned at a comfortable distance and angle.
    • The room will be well-lit to reduce glare.
    • You're encouraged to blink regularly and look away from the screen during breaks.
  • If you experience any discomfort, please inform the researcher immediately.

What to Expect:

  • The entire process will take about 30-45 minutes.
  • You'll be seated comfortably in front of a computer screen.
  • Your data will be kept confidential and used only for this science fair project.

Data Collection:

  • We'll measure the time it takes you to read each passage.
  • Your scores on the comprehension quizzes will be recorded.
  • All data will be anonymized to protect your privacy.

Before We Begin:

  • Please silence your phone and remove any potential distractions.
  • If you have any questions, ask the researcher before starting.
  • Remember, you can withdraw from the study at any time without penalty.

Consent:

By participating, you (and your parent/guardian if you're under 18) agree to have your anonymized data used for this science fair project.
